# Sentry Sound Academy Programme Structure V1

## Purpose

This document defines the academy programme structure.

A programme sits above modules.

Programme
→ Modules
→ SLBs
→ Lessons
→ Assessments
→ Evidence
→ Competency
→ Runtime Execution

This is not yet final certification or NQF mapping.

---

# Core Principle

Sentry Sound Academy should be structured as a training institution with multiple programme pathways.

Each programme should:
- group related modules
- support competency progression
- support future certification
- support role-based learning
- remain standards-ready
- remain platform-aware where applicable

---

# Programme Types

## 1. Foundation Programmes

Purpose:
Introduce learners to the music industry, business basics, digital ecosystems, and platform fundamentals.

Possible programme:
- Sentry Sound Music Industry Fundamentals

Typical modules:
- Music Industry Fundamentals
- Music Business Fundamentals
- Digital Music Ecosystem
- Sentry Sound Platform Fundamentals

---

## 2. Operational Programmes

Purpose:
Train learners to execute real industry workflows.

Possible programme:
- Music Rights, Metadata & Registration Operations

Typical modules:
- Rights & Ownership
- Publishing Administration
- Registrations & Compliance
- Metadata Management
- Royalty Operations
- Distribution Operations

---

## 3. Creative / Technical Programmes

Purpose:
Train learners in production, recording, songwriting, and technical delivery workflows.

Possible programme:
- Music Creation & Production Fundamentals

Typical modules:
- Sound Engineering Fundamentals
- Music Production Workflow
- Songwriting & Composition
- Recording Workflow & Session Evidence
- Audio Delivery Standards

---

## 4. Business / Enterprise Programmes

Purpose:
Train learners in finance, contracts, licensing, artist management, and marketing.

Possible programme:
- Music Business, Finance & Artist Operations

Typical modules:
- Music Finance
- Licensing & Synchronisation
- Contracts in Music
- Artist Management
- Music Marketing & Promotion

---

## 5. Advanced / Governance Programmes

Purpose:
Train advanced learners in compliance, auditability, international operations, governance, and enterprise administration.

Possible programme:
- Music Rights Governance & Enterprise Operations

Typical modules:
- International Collections
- Audit & Compliance
- Governance Workflows
- Multi-Territory Rights Management
- Enterprise Platform Administration

---

# Cross-Programme Platform Thread

The Sentry Sound Platform should appear as a practical operational thread across programmes where relevant.

It may appear as:
- introductory platform module
- practical workflow simulator
- compliance workflow reference
- metadata capture reference
- evidence/audit reference
- royalty tracking reference
- governance reference

Important:
Platform training must align to the real Sentry Sound Platform backend and workflows.

---

# Competency Progression

Programmes should support progression from:

1. Foundation
2. Operational
3. Professional
4. Specialist
5. Governance / Expert

Not every programme needs all five levels at first.

---

# Future Certification Readiness

Each programme should later support:

- programme outcomes
- module outcomes
- SLB outcomes
- assessment criteria
- evidence requirements
- notional hours
- proposed credits
- NQF level consideration
- SAQA/SETA alignment
- international alignment
- moderation readiness

Formal accreditation is not claimed at this stage.
