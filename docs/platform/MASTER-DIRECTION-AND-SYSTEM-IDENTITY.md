## System Identity

Sentry Sound Platform is not just a song registration tool, royalty tracker, compliance app, CRM, metadata manager, or project management tool.

Sentry Sound Platform is a governed music rights operations system.

The core concept is operations.

The platform exists to govern, operationalize, validate, manage, and orchestrate the lifecycle of music rights and related operational assets.

## Core Industry Problem

The music industry is operationally fragmented.

Music rights operations are often managed across spreadsheets, email, WhatsApp, PDFs, disconnected tools, verbal agreements, cloud folders, fragmented metadata systems, and manual registration processes.

This creates rights disputes, missing metadata, ownership conflicts, registration failures, royalty leakage, audit problems, operational inefficiency, evidence gaps, and compliance failures.

Sentry Sound Platform exists to solve rights chaos.

The system must become a single source of governed operational truth for music rights operations.

## Core Platform Pillars

### 1. Rights Capture

Capture and maintain songs, recordings, releases, contributor data, ownership splits, metadata, identifiers, supporting files, and operational context.

### 2. Rights Governance

Govern split validation, contributor confirmation, disputes, amendments, audit history, evidence validation, compliance rules, workflow integrity, and operational approvals.

### 3. Rights Operationalization

Operationalize registrations, submission packaging, export generation, regulator workflows, royalty readiness, evidence preparation, release operations, and compliance execution.

### 4. Rights Intelligence

Future intelligence includes operational analytics, royalty leakage detection, metadata quality analysis, readiness scoring, conflict detection, operational risk detection, AI operational assistance, and workflow intelligence.

## Architectural Philosophy

## Minimum Operational Pipeline

The minimum operational music rights lifecycle is:

1. Create Musical Work
2. Attach Contributors
3. Define Ownership Splits
4. Validate Rights Integrity
5. Attach Evidence
6. Link Recordings and Releases
7. Evaluate Readiness
8. Prepare Registration Package
9. Maintain Audit History
10. Support Amendments and Disputes

This pipeline must become reliable, governed, auditable, recoverable, operationally safe, and extensible before major expansion continues.
