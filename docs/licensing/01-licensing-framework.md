# Licensing Framework

## Ownership principle

Sentry Sound owns its music catalogue unless a contract states otherwise.

## StudyEdge usage

StudyEdge may only use Sentry Sound assets through a formal licence.

## Licence fields to track

Each licence should record:
- asset
- owner
- licensed client
- usage scope
- territory
- duration
- exclusivity
- approval status
- contract document

## Internal sister-company licence

StudyEdge and Sentry Sound may enter into a standing licence agreement that allows StudyEdge to use approved music in:
- lesson players
- educational videos
- marketing videos
- demonstrations
- presentations

## Future sale protection

If StudyEdge is sold, the licence terms must clarify whether the buyer receives:
- continued use rights
- limited use rights
- renewable rights
- no transfer rights

If Sentry Sound is sold, existing StudyEdge licences must remain traceable and enforceable.
