
## Finance Accounts Architecture

Finance accounts are system-level accounts and are separate from contributor ledger balances.

The contributor ledger remains the source of truth for contributor obligations.

Finance accounts will support:
- bank/cash tracking
- liabilities
- revenue recognition
- expense tracking
- payout confirmation
- financial reporting

## Finance Transactions Layer

Added deterministic system-level transaction ledger.

Contributor royalty ledger remains independent.

This layer tracks:
- company cash movement
- liabilities
- expenses
- revenue flows
