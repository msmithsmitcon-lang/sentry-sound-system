# Sentry Sound Academy Standards Alignment Framework V1

## Purpose

This document defines the placeholder framework for future formal standards alignment.

This is not yet formal accreditation.

It prepares every future module, SLB, assessment, and evidence object for structured alignment to:
- SAQA
- SETA
- NQF
- credits
- notional hours
- outcomes-based education
- competency-based training
- international music industry standards

---

# Core Rule

Every module and SLB must be standards-ready from the beginning.

Do not retrofit standards later.

---

# Alignment Fields

Each module and SLB should include placeholders for:

## 1. SAQA Alignment
- SAQA qualification reference
- SAQA unit standard reference
- Exit level outcome relevance
- Specific outcome relevance
- Assessment criteria relevance

## 2. SETA Alignment
- Relevant SETA
- Skills programme relevance
- Occupational pathway relevance
- Workplace evidence relevance
- Provider accreditation relevance

## 3. NQF Alignment
- Proposed NQF level
- Level descriptor relevance
- Complexity level
- Responsibility level
- Autonomy level

## 4. Credits & Notional Hours
- Estimated notional hours
- Contact time estimate
- Self-study time estimate
- Practical/workplace time estimate
- Assessment time estimate
- Proposed credit value

## 5. Outcomes-Based Alignment
- Learning outcomes
- Assessment criteria
- Evidence requirements
- Integrated assessment relevance
- Remediation requirements

## 6. International Standards Alignment
- Music industry standards relevance
- Metadata standards relevance
- Rights administration relevance
- Royalty/reporting standards relevance
- Digital distribution standards relevance
- International certification relevance

---

# Evidence Alignment

Every evidence object should eventually define:

- evidence type
- evidence purpose
- evidence quality criteria
- verification method
- assessor review requirement
- moderation relevance
- audit trail requirement

---

# Assessment Alignment

Every assessment should eventually define:

- assessment method
- competency tested
- assessment criteria
- pass criteria
- reassessment method
- moderation requirement
- evidence linkage

---

# Important Boundary

Do not claim formal accreditation yet.

Do not assign final credits yet.

Do not assign final NQF levels yet.

Do not claim SAQA or SETA approval yet.

This framework only prepares the academy structure for future verification and accreditation work.
