# Module 01 — Music Industry Fundamentals
# SLB Identification V1

## Purpose

This document identifies the foundational competency SLBs for Module 01.

These are not full lessons yet.

These are competency execution blocks that will later support:
- learning
- assessment
- evidence
- remediation
- runtime tracking
- competency state updates

---

# Core Principle

Each SLB must represent:
- a clear competency area
- a measurable understanding
- an assessable concept/workflow
- a future runtime-compatible execution block

---

# SLB Group 1 — Industry Ecosystem Awareness

## SLB 01.01
Introduction to the Modern Music Industry

### Competency Intent
Learner understands the broad structure of the modern music industry ecosystem.

---

## SLB 01.02
Major Industry Role Players

### Competency Intent
Learner can identify major music industry entities and their functions.

Examples:
- artists
- producers
- publishers
- labels
- distributors
- DSPs
- PROs/CMOs

---

## SLB 01.03
Music Industry Career Pathways

### Competency Intent
Learner understands major career pathways and operational roles within the industry.

---
