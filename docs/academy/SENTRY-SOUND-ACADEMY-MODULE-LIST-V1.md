# Sentry Sound Academy Module List V1

## Purpose

This document identifies the first academy module landscape.

It does not define lessons yet.

It exists to structure the academy before detailed curriculum, SLB, assessment, or runtime execution design begins.

---

# Layer 1: Foundation

## Module 1: Music Industry Fundamentals
Introductory understanding of the music industry ecosystem, role players, revenue paths, rights environment, and professional expectations.

## Module 2: Music Business Fundamentals
Basic business literacy for artists, producers, labels, managers, publishers, and music entrepreneurs.

## Module 3: Digital Music Ecosystem
Understanding DSPs, digital distribution, streaming economics, platforms, metadata, and modern music supply chains.

## Module 4: Sentry Sound Platform Fundamentals
Introduction to the Sentry Sound Platform as an operational SaaS system for registrations, compliance, rights, royalties, workflows, and governance.

---

# Layer 2: Operational

## Module 5: Rights & Ownership
Understanding composition rights, master rights, performer rights, publisher shares, split sheets, ownership evidence, and rights conflicts.

## Module 6: Publishing Administration
Understanding publishing roles, work registration, mechanical rights, performance rights, publisher administration, and collection society processes.

## Module 7: Registrations & Compliance
Understanding SAMRO, CAPASSO, SAMPRA, RAV/CIPC, ISWC, ISRC, registration packs, evidence requirements, and compliance statuses.

## Module 8: Royalty Operations
Understanding royalty types, collection flows, reporting, reconciliation, unpaid royalties, adjustments, and royalty tracking.

## Module 9: Metadata Management
Understanding work metadata, recording metadata, contributor metadata, identifiers, usage lists, export packs, and data quality.

## Module 10: Distribution Operations
Understanding release preparation, delivery standards, distributor workflows, takedowns, territory controls, and post-release monitoring.

---

# Layer 3: Technical / Creative

## Module 11: Sound Engineering Fundamentals
Understanding recording, mixing, mastering basics, signal flow, studio roles, and technical production standards.

## Module 12: Music Production Workflow
Understanding beat-making, arrangement, production planning, session management, producer roles, and production documentation.

## Module 13: Songwriting & Composition
Understanding lyrics, melody, harmony, song structure, co-writing, authorship, and composition documentation.

## Module 14: Recording Workflow & Session Evidence
Understanding studio session planning, performer logs, producer confirmations, stems, versions, proof of performance, and evidence packs.

## Module 15: Audio Delivery Standards
Understanding audio formats, loudness, mastering deliverables, metadata tagging, quality control, and platform delivery requirements.

---

# Layer 4: Business / Enterprise

## Module 16: Music Finance
Understanding budgets, recoupment, advances, income streams, expense tracking, VAT/tax considerations, and financial reporting.

## Module 17: Licensing & Synchronisation
Understanding licensing types, sync opportunities, usage permissions, negotiation basics, cue sheets, and rights clearance.

## Module 18: Contracts in Music
Understanding artist agreements, producer agreements, split agreements, publishing agreements, licensing agreements, and contract risk.

## Module 19: Artist Management
Understanding artist development, planning, team coordination, administration, releases, communications, and professional representation.

## Module 20: Music Marketing & Promotion
Understanding audience development, campaigns, content planning, release promotion, brand positioning, and platform analytics.

---

# Layer 5: Advanced / Governance

## Module 21: International Collections
Understanding foreign collection societies, reciprocal agreements, territories, global royalty flows, and cross-border administration.

## Module 22: Audit & Compliance
Understanding audit trails, evidence packs, compliance checks, dispute records, registration proof, and operational accountability.

## Module 23: Governance Workflows
Understanding approval workflows, task routing, permissions, review stages, escalation, and controlled operational execution.

## Module 24: Multi-Territory Rights Management
Understanding territory splits, international ownership, licensing restrictions, publisher mandates, and regional compliance differences.

## Module 25: Enterprise Platform Administration
Understanding workspace administration, user roles, governance settings, reporting, platform controls, and operational oversight.

---
