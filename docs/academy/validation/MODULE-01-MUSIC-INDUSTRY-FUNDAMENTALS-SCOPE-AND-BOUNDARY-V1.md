# Module 01 — Music Industry Fundamentals
# Approved Scope & Boundary V1

## Purpose

This document defines the approved scope, competency direction, and architectural boundary for Module 01.

This prevents:
- module overlap
- competency duplication
- uncontrolled curriculum drift
- technical-domain leakage
- weak foundation structure

This module becomes the foundational industry worldview layer for the academy.

---

# Approved Module Purpose

Music Industry Fundamentals introduces learners to the structure, terminology, operational relationships, business ecosystem, and digital realities of the modern music industry.

The module establishes foundational awareness of:
- creators
- rights
- business structures
- distribution
- revenue flow
- operational workflows
- platform ecosystems
- professional roles
- industry terminology

This module prepares learners for all future academy pathways.

---

# Approved Competency Direction

This module focuses on:

- industry awareness
- terminology understanding
- ecosystem understanding
- workflow awareness
- role awareness
- digital industry awareness
- ownership awareness
- operational awareness

This module does NOT focus on advanced technical production competencies.

# Approved Exclusions

The following areas belong primarily in later modules and should NOT dominate this module:

## Sound Engineering Fundamentals
- deep acoustics

- spectral analysis
- advanced frequency engineering
- advanced dB analysis

## Music Production Workflow

- mixing methodology
- mastering methodology

## Audio Delivery Standards
- loudness standards
- codec delivery
- advanced technical delivery specifications

## Royalty Operations
- advanced royalty calculations
- forensic royalty reconciliation
- enterprise royalty governance

These may be introduced lightly as awareness concepts only.

# Approved Competency Intent

At completion, learners should be able to:

- explain the structure of the music industry
- identify major industry role players
- understand basic rights and ownership concepts
- understand the lifecycle of a music release
- understand how digital distribution works
- understand how royalties and metadata relate to industry operations
- communicate using foundational industry terminology
